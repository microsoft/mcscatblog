Parental Leave Policy

Eligible employees receive up to 16 weeks of paid parental leave for the birth, adoption, or foster placement of a child. Here are the key details:

• Primary caregiver: 16 weeks at full pay
• Secondary caregiver: 6 weeks at full pay
• Leave can be taken in consecutive weeks or split into two blocks within the first 12 months
• Eligible after 12 months of continuous employment
